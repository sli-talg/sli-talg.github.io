Galician Language Tecnologies and Applications

Welcome   

Welcome to the pages of the Research Group on Galician Language Technology and Applications (TALG Group) of the University of Vigo. Here you will find contact information, information about the members of our group and our publications.

Here you will access to several public resources in Galician linguistic technologies developed by TALG, as the SLI machine translation system for Spanish-Galician, the RILG server with integrated language resources for Galician, the OrtoGal free online Galician spell checker, the Multilingual Galnet Dictionary, the Galician Synonym Dictionary, the CLUVI Corpus with parallel texts for Galician language, the CTG - Galician Technical Corpus, the SensoGal Corpus with lemmatised and semantically-tagged parallel texts, the Neoteca application to our Galician neologisms databank, the Aquén consultation tool for Galician toponymic terms, and the Freeling, SLI-Tagger and Lingaliza systems for the automatic linguistic analysis of Galician.

You can check here a full list of our on-line public linguistic resources (also available on GitHub).

Thank you for your interest.
